### Market Opportunity
The property damage assessment market represents a **$12.3 billion annual opportunity** in North America alone, with significant inefficiencies in manual processes, inconsistent reporting standards, and scalability challenges. Our AI-powered platform addresses these pain points with a transformative solution that reduces assessment time by 70% while improving accuracy and professional presentation.

### Target Market Size
- **Total Addressable Market (TAM):** $12.3B (all property damage assessment services)
- **Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM):** $3.8B (digital-forward assessment professionals)
- **Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM):** $380M (3-year capture target)

### Competitive Advantage
We are the **first multi-agent AI platform** specifically designed for property damage assessment, combining computer vision, expert AI agents, and industry expertise to deliver insurance-grade reports in minutes.

### Pricing Strategy

#### **Value-Based Pricing Model**
Our pricing is based on **value delivered** (time savings, increased capacity, professional quality) rather than cost-plus.

**Calculation:**
- Independent adjuster earns $65K/year
- Handles 100 claims/year = $650 per claim
- Each claim takes 8 hours (including assessment)
- Our platform saves 5 hours per assessment
- **Value per claim:** $400 (5 hours × $80/hour)
- **Annual value:** $40,000 (100 claims × $400)

**Pricing Strategy:** Capture 10-20% of value created
- **Annual Value Created:** $40,000
- **Our Price:** $1,788-$4,788/year
- **Chosen Price:** $149/month ($1,788/year) = 4.5% of value
